# Voice Tracking

:::info Pro Feature
Voice tracking requires an Orvana Pro license.
:::

Voice tracking allows presenters to pre-record their links and have them play automatically between songs — giving the sound of a live show with pre-recorded content.

## Recording a Track

1. Open the voice tracking interface
2. Select the gap between two songs
3. Record your link — the outgoing song plays in your left ear, incoming in your right
4. Save — the track is automatically inserted into the schedule
