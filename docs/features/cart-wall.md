# Cart Wall

The cart wall provides instant one-click playback of jingles, sweepers, liners, and sound effects.

## Layout

Carts are arranged in a customizable grid. Each cart shows the title, duration, and color-coded category.

## Hotkeys

Assign keyboard shortcuts to any cart for hands-free triggering during live shows.

## Cart Types

- **Jingle** — plays and stops automatically
- **Loop** — plays continuously until stopped
- **Sweeper** — voice-over over music bed
